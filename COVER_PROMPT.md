# Cover art — ChatGPT / DALL·E prompt

The reader currently ships a typographic **placeholder** cover
(`attached_assets/extrinsic-titles-cover.png`, 1086 × 1448 px, generated by
`tools/make_placeholder_art.py`). Use the prompt below to generate a finished
cover in the NEC house style. When you have an image you like, save it over
`attached_assets/extrinsic-titles-cover.png` (same filename, portrait ~1086 ×
1448 px / 3:4), run `python3 tools/make_placeholder_art.py --og-only` to refresh
the social-share card, commit, and push.

## Main prompt (paste into ChatGPT / DALL·E)

> A premium academic book cover, portrait orientation, 3:4 aspect ratio. Deep
> midnight-navy background (#0d0f26 fading to near-black #04050d) with a very
> subtle cosmic dot-grid and a faint starfield glow behind the center. At the
> top center, a single luminous golden starburst — eight fine rays radiating
> from a bright ivory core, elegant and restrained, like a scholarly imprint
> mark. Below it, a refined symbolic emblem in thin gold line-work: a balanced
> set of scales whose two pans hold, on one side, a small stack of medieval
> coins, and on the other, a flame — justice weighing gain against loss.
> Framing the whole cover, a thin double gold border with small dots at the
> corners. Muted gold (#d6a93a) and warm ivory (#f7f0df) are the only accent
> colors against the dark ground. Mood: sober, luminous, morally serious, a
> cross between a Renaissance treatise and a modern finance monograph. Elegant,
> minimal, lots of negative space, high-contrast, no clutter, no people, no
> photographic realism — clean vector-like line illustration and soft glows
> only.

## Title text

DALL·E often garbles lettering, so consider generating the artwork **without
text** and setting the title in afterward, or generate a version with text and
keep the cleanest one. If you want the model to attempt the text, append:

> Set in the lower third, in an elegant serif (Trajan / Garamond feel): the
> title **CAPITAL, RISK, AND THE SIN OF USURY** in ivory small-caps, and beneath
> it, in gold italic, the subtitle *The Forgotten History of the Extrinsic
> Titles*. Above the artwork, small gold letter-spaced caps: A WORKING
> MANUSCRIPT.

## Palette + specs (for consistency with the reader and its siblings)

- Background navy: `#0d0f26` → `#08091a` → `#04050d` (vertical gradient)
- Gold accent: `#d6a93a` (and a dimmer `#c9a03a`)
- Ivory text: `#f7f0df`
- Portrait, ~1086 × 1448 px (3:4). Keep the composition centered so it crops
  cleanly for the Open Graph card.

## Alternative emblem ideas (swap into the prompt if you prefer)

- **The four titles as four small gold icons** in a row or quadrant: a broken
  clock or hourglass (poena conventionalis / delay), a cracked coin or wound
  (damnum emergens / actual loss), a road forking away (lucrum cessans /
  foregone gain), and a ship on waves or dice (periculum sortis / risk).
- **A single Roman coin** dissolving into light at its edge — money that serves
  life rather than extracts it.
- **An open medieval ledger** with a faint golden halo, quill resting across it.
